ABOUT CONNECTICON

Connecticon


Connecticon is a website that connects people. It is a place where people can interact with each other, by creating channels. The channels can be public or private according to the necessity of the people. People also get recommended about the channels according to the most visited priority.

Here, people can even post their issues, thus get them resolved as soon as possible. People also get updated about the ongoing events in the college through our News page.

People can even share their views through the Radio page, where their literature, poems will be shared. Thus this is a website that helps everyone to interact with their mates.
